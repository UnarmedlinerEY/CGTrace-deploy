Where CGTrace wins
Sharesight is built for investors, not accountants. It has no multi-client firm view, no ATO audit trail, and its corporate action engine lacks the depth required for compliance-grade output. Every dimension that matters to an accounting firm is a CGTrace advantage.
Where Sharesight wins — for now
Broker integrations and securities coverage are real gaps at launch. Sharesight has 240+ broker APIs and 700k+ securities built over a decade. These are Phase 2–3 investments — not year-one blockers for the accounting firm segment, who onboard client data manually anyway.
